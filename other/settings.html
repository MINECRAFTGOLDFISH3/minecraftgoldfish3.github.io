Settings

Tab Cloaker:

Sets the title and favicon of the site to something different. Expires in 30 days.

Title Changer

Enter the title of the site you want to appear.

Favicon Changer

Enter a web url of a .png or .ico file you want to appear as the favicon of the site. (Example: https://www.google.com/favicon.ico)


Save/Load your Data

Saving your game data:

Your actions on the site are automatically saved as cookies.

To manually save your data, click on the button below.

Loading your game data:

Click on your 'data.txt' file and edit it. Select all the text. Then, copy this text. Paste it in the textbox, and click load.

Warning: this will override all current game data. Make sure that you are loading the correct file.
